Energy News Network reported on Duke Energy's proposal to build new gas
combustion turbines as a bridge fuel in its carbon transition plan — one of
the core issues addressed in Tyler's expert testimony at the North Carolina
Utilities Commission. The piece examined the tension between Duke's
decarbonization commitments and its gas plant ambitions.
