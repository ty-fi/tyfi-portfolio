Charlotte Business Journal coverage (byline: Deon Roberts) of an updated
carbon stranding analysis finding that Duke Energy's revised integrated
resource plan continued to expose North and South Carolina ratepayers to
substantial stranded asset risk from new gas plant construction, even after
Duke made modifications to the original 2020 plan.
