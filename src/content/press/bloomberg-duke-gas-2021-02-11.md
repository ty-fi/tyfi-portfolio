Bloomberg's coverage of the tension in Duke Energy's carbon strategy —
planning to build new gas plants while also committing to early retirement
of those same plants — drew on Tyler's recently released Carbon Stranding
report. The piece highlighted the financial risk this poses to ratepayers
and the analytical gap in Duke's planning process.
