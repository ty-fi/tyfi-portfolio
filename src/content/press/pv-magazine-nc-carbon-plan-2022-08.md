PV Magazine USA covered the headline finding of Synapse's Carbon-Free by 2050
modeling report: that a high-renewables clean energy portfolio would save North
Carolina ratepayers approximately $18 billion through 2050 compared to Duke
Energy's proposed carbon plan. The coverage ran ahead of Tyler's September 2022
direct testimony in the NCUC Carbon Plan proceeding.
