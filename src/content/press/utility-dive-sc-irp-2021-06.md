Utility Dive reported on the South Carolina Public Service Commission's final
order in the 2020 IRP proceeding, which rejected significant portions of Duke
Energy's proposed long-term generation plan — the outcome Tyler's expert
testimony and Vote Solar's advocacy had sought. The proceeding concluded
in June 2021 following Tyler's direct testimony (February 2021) and
surrebuttal testimony (April 2021).
