Authored briefing on behalf of Vote Solar examining the financial and
systemic risks of carbon stranding in Duke Energy's Modified 2020
Integrated Resource Plan. The briefing analyzes the potential for
under-utilization of fossil generation assets as state and utility
decarbonization commitments advance, and the implications for ratepayers,
credit ratings, and resource planning.

Filed: April 2021
On behalf of: Vote Solar
Context: Filed alongside expert testimony in the South Carolina Energy
Freedom Act proceeding concerning Duke Energy Carolinas and Duke Energy
Progress IRPs.
