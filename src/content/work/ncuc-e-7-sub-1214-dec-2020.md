Direct testimony filed jointly with James Van Nostrand on behalf of Vote Solar
before the North Carolina Utilities Commission in Duke Energy Carolinas' rate
case. The testimony addressed rate design issues, energy efficiency, and the
alignment of Duke's rate structure with clean energy and affordability goals
in North Carolina.
