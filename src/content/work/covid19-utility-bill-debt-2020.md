A Vote Solar report projecting the magnitude of residential utility bill debt
accumulation during the COVID-19 pandemic — among the first quantitative
analyses of this problem to reach policymakers. The report modeled expected
arrears based on unemployment projections and historical utility debt data,
and proposed a framework for state utility regulators to address the crisis
in a way that protects both customers and utility financial stability.
