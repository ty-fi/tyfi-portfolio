Co-authored contribution to RMI's _Power Shift_ report, a comprehensive look
at how the U.S. electric grid is being re-architected for the clean energy
transition. Covers shifts in generation mix, the rise of distributed energy
resources, transmission expansion needs, and the institutional changes
required of utilities and regulators.
