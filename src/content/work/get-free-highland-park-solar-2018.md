A Dow Sustainability Report examining the economic and policy landscape for
community solar development in Highland Park, Michigan, a low-income Detroit
enclave with high energy burden and aging utility infrastructure. The report
analyzed the economics of community solar under multiple ownership and
financing models and recommended policy and program design elements to
ensure benefits reach the community's residents.
