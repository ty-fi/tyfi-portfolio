Direct testimony filed on behalf of Vote Solar with the South Carolina
Public Service Commission addressing Duke Energy Carolinas' and Duke
Energy Progress' 2020 Integrated Resource Plans. The testimony examined
the IRPs' alignment with state energy policy, cost-effectiveness of
proposed clean energy and fossil portfolios, and implications for
ratepayers in the Carolinas.
