RMI analysis examining Wisconsin as a case study in gas turbine over-commitment,
drawing parallels to the coal plant stranded asset problem of the prior decade.
As supply chain delays extend gas turbine lead times to 4-7 years, utilities
are locking in long-term commitments to fossil infrastructure that may become
uneconomic before the end of its useful life — mirroring the stranded asset
exposure this work documented years earlier at [[carbon-stranding-full-report-2021]].
