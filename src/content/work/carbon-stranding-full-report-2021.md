Vote Solar report examining the risk that Duke Energy's proposed generation
portfolio — particularly planned new gas plants — will become uneconomic
before the end of its useful life as corporate carbon neutrality commitments,
state carbon requirements, and falling clean energy costs converge. The report
quantified the potential stranded asset exposure for Duke Energy Carolinas and
Duke Energy Progress customers across a range of decarbonization scenarios.

The report received prominent coverage at launch, including Bloomberg, S&P
Global, GreenTechMedia, Charlotte Business Journal, E&E News, and Utility
Dive. It was foundational to Vote Solar's subsequent regulatory interventions
in North and South Carolina.
