Direct testimony filed jointly with James Van Nostrand on behalf of
Vote Solar in the Virginia State Corporation Commission docket concerning
Dominion Energy Virginia's integrated resource plan. The testimony
analyzed Dominion's resource mix decisions, with attention to
cost-effectiveness, clean energy deployment, and consistency with
Virginia's clean energy policy framework.

Filed: April 13, 2020
Before: Virginia State Corporation Commission
On behalf of: Vote Solar
Employer at filing: Synapse Energy Economics
