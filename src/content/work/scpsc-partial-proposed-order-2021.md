A partial proposed order filed with the South Carolina Public Service
Commission proposing specific findings of fact, conclusions of law, and
ordering paragraphs for the commission to adopt in its final order on the
2020 Integrated Resource Plans of Duke Energy Carolinas and Duke Energy
Progress. The proposed order emphasized the need for the commission to
address carbon risk exposure, the inadequacy of Duke's clean energy
commitments, and the implications of the South Carolina Energy Freedom Act
for utility resource planning.

Filed: June 2021
Before: South Carolina Public Service Commission
On behalf of: Vote Solar
Dockets: 2019-224-E, 2019-225-E
