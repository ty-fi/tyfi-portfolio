Direct testimony filed jointly with James Van Nostrand on behalf of Vote Solar
before the North Carolina Utilities Commission in Duke Energy Progress's rate
case. The testimony addressed rate design, energy efficiency, and grid
modernization, advocating for structures that would facilitate clean energy
deployment and protect low-income customers.

Filed: April 13, 2020
Before: North Carolina Utilities Commission
On behalf of: Vote Solar
