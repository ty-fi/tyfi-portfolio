Initial public comments filed on behalf of Vote Solar with the South
Carolina Public Service Commission. The comments addressed measures to
mitigate the impacts of threats to safe and reliable utility service,
including those arising from extreme weather events, fuel supply
disruptions, and the transition of the generation fleet.
