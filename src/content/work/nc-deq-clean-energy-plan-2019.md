Comments filed on behalf of Vote Solar with the North Carolina Department of
Environmental Quality on the agency's draft Clean Energy Plan. The comments
provided technical analysis supporting more ambitious clean energy targets,
integrated resource planning reforms, and utility accountability mechanisms.
The Clean Energy Plan process was a precursor to North Carolina's landmark
House Bill 951 clean energy legislation.
