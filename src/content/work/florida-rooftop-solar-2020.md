A Vote Solar report assessing the current state of rooftop solar in Florida:
deployment levels, economics at current rates, barriers to adoption, and policy
levers available to the Florida Public Service Commission and legislature.
The report was developed in the context of Florida net metering and rate design
proceedings where investor-owned utilities were seeking to reduce the value of
customer-sited solar.
