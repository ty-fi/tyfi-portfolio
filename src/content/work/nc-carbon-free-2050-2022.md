Quantitative analysis examining how North Carolina's power sector can meet
the carbon-free requirements of House Bill 951 at least cost to ratepayers.
Using Synapse's EnCompass model, the report evaluated a range of clean energy
portfolios and found that aggressive clean energy deployment — including
expanded solar, battery storage, offshore wind, and energy efficiency —
consistently outperformed Duke Energy's proposed plan on cost and emissions.
