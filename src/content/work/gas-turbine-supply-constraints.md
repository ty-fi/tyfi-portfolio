Lead-authored analysis from RMI's Carbon-Free Electricity practice showing
that lead times for new gas turbines have stretched to 4-7 years, creating
a near-term reliability risk for utilities counting on new gas to meet load
growth. The article lays out a near-term toolkit — energy efficiency,
demand response, accelerated renewables, transmission upgrades, and
repurposing of existing fossil units — that can close the gap at lower cost.
