A Vote Solar framework document published in advance of Duke Energy's 2020
integrated resource plan filings, articulating ten principles for evaluating
whether the IRP serves the public interest. Principles addressed clean energy
deployment, carbon risk transparency, ratepayer affordability, grid resilience,
equity, and stakeholder engagement.
