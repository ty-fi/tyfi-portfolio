Vote Solar analysis of the economic risks Florida utility customers face as a
result of the state's heavy reliance on natural gas for power generation.
The report examined fuel price volatility, the risk that new gas infrastructure
will become stranded under decarbonization scenarios, and the relative cost
trajectories of solar and storage as alternatives. Published as part of
Vote Solar's intervention in Florida utility rate and resource planning cases.
