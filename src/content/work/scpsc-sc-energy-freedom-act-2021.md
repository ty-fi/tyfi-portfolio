Surrebuttal testimony filed with the South Carolina Public Service Commission
in the proceeding related to the South Carolina Energy Freedom Act and the
integrated resource plans of Duke Energy Carolinas and Duke Energy Progress.
The testimony engaged with rebuttal arguments on carbon risk, stranded
asset exposure, and the cost-effectiveness of clean energy portfolios for
South Carolina ratepayers.

Before: South Carolina Public Service Commission
On behalf of: Vote Solar
