A Vote Solar brief articulating principles for state public utility commissions
to guide their regulatory responses to the surge in residential utility debt
caused by COVID-19. Principles addressed moratoriums on disconnections,
amortization of arrears, cost recovery, equity considerations, and utility
reporting requirements.
