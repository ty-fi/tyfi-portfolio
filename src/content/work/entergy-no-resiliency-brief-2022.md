A brief intended for regulators, consumer advocates, and other decisionmakers
participating in Entergy New Orleans' grid resiliency planning process. The
brief outlines principles for evaluating resiliency investments, including
the need for rigorous cost-benefit analysis, equitable distribution of
resiliency benefits, and integration of climate-related physical risks into
long-range planning.
