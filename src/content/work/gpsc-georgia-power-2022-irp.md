Direct testimony filed on behalf of Sierra Club with the Georgia Public
Service Commission in the Georgia Power 2022 Integrated Resource Plan
and 2022 Demand-Side Management Plan proceedings. Synapse provided
EnCompass-based modeling identifying shortcomings of Georgia Power's
proposed IRP and recommended a portfolio with substantially expanded
energy efficiency.

The Georgia PSC issued an order on July 29, 2022 adopting a stipulation
that approved expanded energy efficiency — one of the central
recommendations made in this testimony.
