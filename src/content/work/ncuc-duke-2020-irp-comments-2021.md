Initial public comments filed on behalf of Vote Solar with the North Carolina
Utilities Commission in the proceeding on Duke Energy Carolinas' and Duke
Energy Progress' 2020 Integrated Resource Plans. The comments raised concerns
about Duke's carbon risk exposure, the cost of new gas plant construction, and
the commission's responsibility to consider climate-related financial risks in
evaluating utility resource planning.

Filed: Early 2021
Before: North Carolina Utilities Commission
On behalf of: Vote Solar
