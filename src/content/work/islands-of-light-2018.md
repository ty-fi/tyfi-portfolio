Journal article published in the Agora Planning Journal (Volume 12, pp. 74-80)
examining the public-good dimensions of microgrid technology. The piece
analyzed how microgrids are typically deployed as private infrastructure for
anchor institutions or commercial districts, and explored policy and planning
frameworks that could direct microgrid development toward broader community
resilience and energy access goals.
