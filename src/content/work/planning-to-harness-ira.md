How the IRA's production and investment tax credits, bonus credits for
domestic content, energy communities, and low-income communities can be
modeled in utility planning processes. The report provides a framework for
utilities and their stakeholders to capture the full value of IRA incentives
in IRP analyses, ratepayer impact estimates, and procurement decisions.
