Direct testimony filed before the Virginia State Corporation Commission in
the proceeding on Dominion Energy Virginia's application to establish an
experimental residential time-of-use rate. The testimony evaluated the
proposed rate design against principles of cost-reflectivity, customer
equity, and potential impacts on solar adoption and electric vehicle adoption.
